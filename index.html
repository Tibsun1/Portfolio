Projects

I've worked with a few hobbyist teams on various projects in my journey. Fractured Alliance is my current and most developed project. Fractured Alliance is a competitive RTS inspired by the classics in the genre like Red Alert and Starcraft. We have a playable demo campaign mission and multiplayer skirmishes with two different factions.

Unit controls and pathfinding have been my main focus on this project. The complexities of networking player actions while making units respond quickly, reliably, and predictably has been extremely challenging and rewarding. I've also had the opportunity to work on custom shaders and get familiar with HLSL in order to display the minimap and fog of war.
